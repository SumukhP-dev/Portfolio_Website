Wordle Week iOS App

WordleWeek is a SwiftUI-based word game where players guess a hidden five-letter word within six attempts. Built solo, the app features an interactive 6Ã—5 grid, a dynamic on-screen keyboard, animated letter tiles, and color-coded feedback for guesses.
