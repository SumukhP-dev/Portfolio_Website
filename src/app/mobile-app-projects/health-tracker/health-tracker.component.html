HealthTracker App

HealthTrack is a personalized health and wellness mobile app designed to empower users in achieving their fitness and lifestyle goals. The app integrates fitness tracking, nutrition tools, health metric monitoring (heart rate, sleep, calories), and community engagement features into a single platform. Users gain access to guided sessions, articles, and challenges to support their health journey.

Developed by CS2340A Team 4 in Summer 2024, the project followed an Agile methodology with sprint cycles, implementing MVVM architecture, design patterns (Singleton, Observer, Strategy, GRASP, SOLID), and unit testing with JUnit to ensure quality and scalability.
