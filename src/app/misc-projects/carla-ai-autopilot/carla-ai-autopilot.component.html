CARLA Autopilot Multimodal LLM/Vision Transformer AI Model

CARLA Autopilot Multimodal LLM/Vision Transformer AI Model is a multimodal AI project that integrates vision, audio, and language models to enable autonomous navigation in a simulated environment. Using a Vision Transformer for image-to-text, speech-to-text for audio input, and Google Gemini for reasoning, the system generates real-time driving instructions to control a CARLA-simulated car. This project demonstrates expertise in multimodal AI, autonomous vehicle research, and LLM-based decision-making.
