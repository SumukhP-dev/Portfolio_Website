CARLA Autopilot Multimodal LLM/Vision Transformer Model

The CARLA Autopilot Multimodal LLM/Vision Transformer Model is a sophisticated autonomous driving system that integrates Vision Transformer models, speech recognition, and Large Language Models (LLMs) to enable intelligent vehicle control in the CARLA simulator. The system processes multimodal inputs (camera images and audio commands) to generate real-time driving instructions.

The system uses a multimodal fusion strategy where the Vision Transformer analyzes camera frames to generate scene descriptions, speech recognition converts voice commands to text, and the Gemini LLM synthesizes these inputs along with current vehicle state to generate appropriate speed and steering values. This project demonstrates expertise in multimodal AI, autonomous vehicle research, and LLM-based decision-making.
